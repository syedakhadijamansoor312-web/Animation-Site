What is Animation?

  • Animation is the art of bringing images,text,or objects to life by creating the illusion of movement.

  • Animation transforms static designs into interactive stories.

  • Animation is used to make content more engaging,interactive,and visually appealing.

  • Animation adds life to your ideas and motion to your message.

Animation Includes:

Pre-Production

This stage involves storytelling, scriptwriting, and storyboarding. It sets the visual foundation.

Production

The execution phase where characters are modeled and animated. It brings ideas to life through motion.

Post-Production

The final stage includes compositing and sound design. It renders the project into a professional format.
